Fittings work i do

Doors

When it comes to installing doors nothing is a problem for me, we can remove previous doors and doorframes and fit entirely new doors and frames. We also can paint and varnish doors and doorhandles to better fit their environments.

Bathrooms

we can completley rip out all of the previous bathrooms and replace them, they can be replaced with any bathroom of your choosing. if you would only like select items removed or select items installed we can do that.

Kitchens

If we are fitting a kitchen for you we can get one custom made for you from howdens. it dosn't matter if you already have a kitchen installed as we can rip out all of it and remove or keep any parts of the kitchen you like. As we get custom fit kitchens we can build and fit them into different any space you like.

Flooring

When it comes to flooring we do all types of flooring. We can fit solid wood floorboards, vinyl, laminate and any other type of flooring you would like. We also rip up and dispose of old flooring if required by the client.
